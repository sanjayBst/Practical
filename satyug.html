Satyug Darshan

Overview

Satyug Darshan Technical Campus is situated in Faridabad in Haryana state of India. it is accredited from AICTE, DTE and it is affiliated to YMCA University of Science and Technology. SATYUG, Faridabad offers 9 courses across 2 streams namely Engineering, Management and across 3 degrees like B.Tech, BBA, PGD. SATYUG campus is spread over 11 Acres. Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as A/C, AV Lab, Auditorium, ATM, Canteen, Computer Lab, Gym, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room are also there.
